Crytals Collector!!!

How to play!!

The player will be shown a random number(between 19 - 120) at the start of the game.

The values of the stone will be kept hidden from player, so goodluck guessing the values.

Each of the infinity stones hold a random value between 1 - 12, that will change after each round.

The player wins if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game.

The player loses if their score goes above the random number.

The game restarts whenever the player wins or loses.
